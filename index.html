Kuang Gong, Ph.D Candidate [Curriculum vitae] B.S, Information Science and Electronic Engineering, Zhejiang University M.S, Statistics, University of California, Davis Email: kugong. at. ucdavis.edu About Me I am a Ph.D candidate in Department of Biomedical Engineering, UC DAVIS, working on medical image reconstruction under the supervision of Prof. Jinyi Qi. I received my B.S from Information Science and Electronic Engineering (ISEE) from Zhejiang University in 2011 and M.S in statistics from UC DAVIS in 2015 .
